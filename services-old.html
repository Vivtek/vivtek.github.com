Services

  • Internet/intranet consulting ($75/hour)
    We can help you put the Internet (or your intranet) to work in ways you may never have considered. Our primary strengths in this regard are tight integration of database and HTTP server, and programmable content management tools (so you can update your site without editing HTML). We approach Internet development from the perspective of years of experience with real-world programming, not just as a part-time adjunct to graphical design.

    We can provide you with system components based on AOLserver/Tcl, Microsoft NT/IIS/ASP, or generic Unix or Windows CGI scripts in C, C++, or Perl. We can also help you make decisions about which technologies to use.

    If you're thinking about electronic commerce and are intending to use CyberCash, then see our CyberCash topic page.

  • Electronic commerce service
    We provide an electronic commerce service on our server for small businesses. This integrates seamlessly with your existing site and starts at $20/month.

    Please visit our electronic commerce page for more information.

  • Website hosting
    We can also provide Web hosting to a limited number of sites. We're interested in sites with unusual needs and specialize in database-backed sites, of course. Starts at $10/month depending on your needs. Click here for details.

  • Windows internals and software systems integration ($80/hour)
    Need a custom COM/ActiveX component? We can write it for you; we've done some really scary ones. We know how COM works.

    Or maybe you just need to encapsulate some functionality in a DLL. Or maybe you aren't even sure yet what you need. Don't worry, this isn't really all that uncommon. We can help you understand what your systems integration needs are, and we can help you implement integration. Unlike many software development organizations, we understand that your investment in existing systems can be considerable and we find it a challenge to use what is already in place. We actually like maintenance!

  • Document management ($100/hour for Mezz/Panagon)
    We have worked with Saros Mezzanine and now FileNET Panagon for years. If you haven't heard of them, these are electronic document management (EDM) tools for medium to large business units. We can provide custom EDM integration with your software, existing or new. If you're a smaller business, that's OK. We can make some good recommendations for the smaller group (or you as an individual) as well, and we can work with you to make your document management professional and reliable.

    If you are working with Mezzanine or Panagon, please visit our Mezzanine topic page, or the new Panagon topic page.

    We're also working on a general document management and version control focus page, and you'll be the first to know when it's ready to go.

  • Database programming, workflow programming ($75/hour)
    We have extensive experience with database programming in various forms. Whether you are wanting something to back a website, or just an Access database to keep track of stuff, we can help. We've been there.

  • General programming ($75/hour)
    Last but not least, our catchall category. This covers "everything else." We can do Visual Basic frontend applications, we can do behind-the-scenes Perl scripts to process your operational data, and anything in between.
