Bingo card generator generator

id:2
title:Bingo card generator generator

Allow people to enter a name for a bingo card, then enter any number of texts for the bingo card. Then have a Perl script to retrieve 25 texts (repeating if necessary) in a random order, and put them into a table.

Example: Making Light troll bingo, or creationist apology bingo, or ... what was the other one? I can't remember, but a third one occurred to me yesterday.

This would require a modification to DataWiki permitting parameterized data entry forms (i.e. we'd have one list of bingo cards with add/mod/del, but then for each card we'd have a sublist (parameterized in a second table with a linking column) with a local list/add/mod/del.)
