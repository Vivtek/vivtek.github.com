Glasnost and perestroika

Vivtek is committed to changing the software industry. Our customer model is radically different from those of other companies. We give away knowledge for free, we make our products as cheap as possible to support our operation, and we involve the customer. Scan on down the page to see how new this really is, and take a look at any of our product home pages for more.

But it's not all just perestroika. As a look at a product home page will also tell you, we strive to be completely forthright about our products. We tell you exactly what people like and don't like, and what we are trying to do about it. We give you a rundown of the products in competition with ours, and why we think you might like ours anyway. We'll tell you our prices right on the page, because we have nothing to fear.

Nor do you. We have a money-back guarantee on our products. If you don't like it, don't keep it. But tell us why! We'd like to change it, after all.

Source code availability

Even better, the full source code of each product is browsable from its home page, using our own literate programming facility. This means that while you can't use the code in your own projects, you certainly can see how things are done, and you can very easily assess the complexity of enhancements yourself. This greatly offsets the expense of customization, so that we can offer customized versions of products very easily. And if for any reason we decide to discontinue a product, or if Vivtek should become defunct (hey, we're not stupid, it's the nineties and these things happen), then you will get the full source code of any Vivtek product affected, delivered via email.

Cash back

Besides knowing at any time the exact planned schedule for bug fixes and enhancements to products, you have even more involvement at Vivtek, and we mean real involvement. When you purchase a Vivtek product, you become a part owner and a distributor of that product. This means that you first of all get a small portion of every subsequent sale of that product, and that you get a larger portion of the sale price of any copy of that product which you actively sold. Since all Vivtek products have freely distributable versions, this means that this is shareware with a vengeance. Go ahead and share it as often as you like, and get some cash back for a change.

But there's more. If you are the first person to discover a bug in a Vivtek product, you get a bonus portion of sales. If an enhancement that you propose later becomes incorporated in a standard version of a product, you are part owner of the enhancement, and you get a proportional share of sales. If you propose a whole new product, the same thing follows. In all cases, of course, we decide the proportions (see the legalese on this).

And there's even more! If you join our distributed developer program (the developer isn't distributed, just the ... oh, never mind, you know what we mean), then you can even make changes to our products, download those customized versions, and test them on your own site. In all cases, we track the changes and do the compilation, and we own copyright to the change, but if you fix a bug, you get paid. If we sell your enhancement, you get paid. And if someone has posted a bounty on a bug, you get paid even more.
