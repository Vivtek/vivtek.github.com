The Vivtek Industry Survey
V A P O R W A R E   W A R N I N G

The Vivtek Industry Survey is something we want to do when we have time. We're sure you understand. What it will be is an ontological database of terms and concepts from the software industry and theoretical programming; basically a formalization of the topic list that we maintain right now. However, this is kind of long-term. If you're interested in helping (this would be on a voluntary basis) then please contact us; we've got some good ideas that could be worth a thesis or two if you're an academic, or worth a nice resume blurb in any case. And it will be cool stuff.
