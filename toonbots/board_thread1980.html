Don't get it? Dont GET it?

Beheaded dead communists and hyperbolic contemporary capitalists bantering and rhyming in Seussian prose? Metacartoonist fifth-wall humor while exploiting the nature of the toon-o-matic construct? Vague forum in-circle humor with wolverines and minimalist characters?

"Well you see... it's like... well, that's Lenin and that's Dubya and.... uh... nevermind"
