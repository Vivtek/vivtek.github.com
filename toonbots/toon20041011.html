Where did my Google go?
Toonbots archive - episode published 20041011


First toon    Previous toon    Next toon    Today's toon
As it is a truism that obscurity reflects perspicacity and innovation, the metacartoonist demonstrates that his new obscurity technique is unstoppable: as the Taino petroglyph is a relatively fresh icon in the Internet's memetic context, and yet simultaneously arrives with a thousand years of iconographic association unknown to the median ursine (irregardless of hattedness), it bears an unescapable parallelism to the metacartooning framework itself; the use, then, of such an icon of unbridled obscurity can only serve to enlighten the careful devotee of the Metacartooning Tao.
