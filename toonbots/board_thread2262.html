PAFTWJ Anthem #2: An Ode to Michael

Presenting another attempt at a Jihad anthem, once again inspired by a song from the musical version of Les Miserables ("Master of the House," and a midi file for the tune can be found here: http://home.netvigator.com/~misaki/master.mid). This time, an ode to Michael himself! (I hope this formats correctly the first time, for once.)

Master of the Dot,
Keeper of the Perl,
Steps aside when Nappy wants to window hurl,

Kind father and spouse,
Meta-cartoonist,
Able to mine humor from dead Communists,

Updates whene'er he pleases,
Writing code behind the scenes,
Continually teases with loud cries of "NEXT: WOLVERINES!"

Master of the Dot,
Pokey devotee,
Revels in his meta-fictionality.

Works in his basement,
There for all to see,
Busier than all the apes of Hungary!

Yes, the Jihad all love Michael,
Though we are a surly lot.
Everybody give a hand,
From across this meta-land,
Everybody give a hand to the master of the dot!
