Toonbots -- ALL YOUR BASE ARE BELONG TO US

[ toonbots home ] [ the toon-o-matic ] [ forum ]
So I thought I'd have a little fun with Babelfish. If you somehow haven't heard of Babelfish, it's a Web-accessible translation program. It's based on SYSTRAN, arguably the very first machine translation software, which has a continuous history dating back to the 1940's, where it was started as a Russian-English translator in Dayton, Ohio. Ah, the heady days when we could still believe that human cognition was going to be easy to emulate!

So you thought ALL YOUR BASE ARE BELONG TO US is funny, eh? Try translating it to German. Here's the original:

IN A.D. 2051 WAR BREAK OUT.
WE HAVE SIGNAL.
MAIN SCREEN TURN ON.
IT'S YOU!
HELLO GENTLEMEN HOW ARE YOU?
ALL YOUR BASE ARE BELONG TO US.
YOU HAVE NO CHANCE TO SURVIVE MAKE YOUR TIME.
WHAT YOU SAY?
MOVE ALL 'ZIG'.
As usual, I don't quote all that accurately. But most of it's there, you'll agree. But here's the German "equivalent."
IM KRIEG A.D. 2051 BRECHEN SIE AUS.
WIR HABEN SIGNAL.
MAIN BILDSCHIRM SCHALTEN EIN.
ES IST SIE!
HALLO HERREN, WIE GEHT ES IHNEN?
IHRE GANZE UNTERSEITE SIND GEHOEREN NACH US.
SIE HABEN KEINE WAHRSCHEINLICHKEIT ZU UEBERLEBEN BILDEN IHRE ZEIT.
WAS SAGEN SIE?
VERSCHIEBEN SIE ALLES 'ZIG'.
OK, so maybe you don't speak German. I do, and this was mildly amusing; "base" is translated as "underside", stuff like that. Then I thought, hey, let's let it come back to English. You can often get Systran to do really funny stuff this way (yes, I did a readings-and-research on machine translation a few years back and I'm still rather fixated on it.)
IN THE WAR A.D. 2051 BREAKING IT OUT.
WE CREDIT SIGNAL.
MAIN DISPLAY SWITCHING.
HELLO GENTLEMEN, HOW ARE YOU?
THEIR WHOLE LOWER SURFACE ARE HEARING TO US.
IT CREDIT NO PROBABILITY TO SURVIVING FORMING YOUR TIME.
WHICH LEGENDS IT?
SHIFT ALL 'ZIG'.
"Legends"? Yeah. The word "sagen" means "they (or you) say", and "Sagen" means "legends". I'm guessing the all-caps are confusing it. I like "THEIR WHOLE LOWER SURFACE", too.

So let's take another turn, shall we? Yeah, this is just starting to get good.

IM KRIEG A.D. 2051 ES HERAUS BRECHEND.
WIR SCHREIBEN SIGNAL GUT.
MAIN BILDSCHIRMANZEIGECSchaltung.
SIE IST SIE!
HALLO HERREN, WIE GEHT ES IHNEN?
IHRE VOLLSTAENDIGE UNTERSEITE SIND HOERFAEHIGKEIT NACH US.
SIE SCHREIBT KEINE WAHRSCHEINLICHKEIT ZUM UEBERLEBEN GUT, IHRE ZEIT BILDEND.
WELCHE LEGENDEN ES?
VERSCHIEBEN SIE ALLES 'ZIG'.
You wish you understood German at this point, because some of this is really getting hilarious. Let's see what survives into English. I will separate that Schaltung up there where the 'C' crept in. (Don't they ever debug this stuff? Answer: no. Literally, no. No-one has understood the Systran code, ever, and since the original programmers are in their nineties or dead, they couldn't help much anyway.)
IN THE WAR A.D. 2051 IT OUT BREAKING.
WE WRITING SIGNAL PROPERTY MAIN SCREEN DISPLAY circuit.
THEM ARE THEM!
HELLO GENTLEMEN, HOW ARE YOU?
THEIR COMPLETE LOWER SURFACE ARE AUDITION TO US.
IT DOES NOT WRITE PROBABILITY FOR SURVIVING PROPERTY, YOUR TIME IN AN EDUCATED MANNER.
WHICH LEGENDS IT?
SHIFT ALL 'ZIG'.
I'm driven to see where this is going. To save some typing, I'm going to eliminate those sentences which survive intact (HELLO GENTLEMEN) or which have reached a bistable state (SHIFT ALL 'ZIG') and just go with the ones which show more ... schizoid tendencies.
IM KRIEG A.D. 2051 ES AUS DEM BRECHEN.
WIR SCHREIBEN SIGNAL-CEigenschaft MAIN BILDSCHIRM-CBildschirmanzeige-Stromkreis.
SIE SIND SIE!
IHRE KOMPLETTE UNTERSEIT SIND HOERPROBE NACH US. SIE SCHREIBT WAHRSCHEINLICHKEIT NICHT FUER UEBERLEBEN Eigenschaft, IHRE ZEIT IN EINER GEBILDETEN WEISE.
WELCHE LEGENDEN ES?
Where is this taking us? (That "Eigenschaft" (property) keeps popping up makes me believe that it's an internal tag for an attribute.) Wash, rinse, and repeat:
IN THE WAR A.D. 2051 IT FROM BREAKING.
WE WRITING SIGNAL characteristic MAIN BILDSCHIRM screen display electric circuit.
THEIR COMPLETE LOWER SURFACE ARE HEARING SAMPLE TO US.
IT DOES NOT WRITE PROBABILITY FOR SURVIVING characteristic, YOUR TIME IN a FORMED WAY.
Yeah. THEIR COMPLETE LOWER SURFACE ARE HEARING SAMPLE TO US. Suddenly that nameless Japanese copy editor seems a lot more human, right? OK. I can keep this up all night:
IM KRIEG A.D. 2051 ES VOM BRECHEN.

SIE SCHREIBT WAHRSCHEINLICHKEIT NICHT FUER UEBERLEBENEIGENSCHAFT, IHRE ZEIT IN einer FORMS WEISE.
What?!? That's ... not German. Exactly. I particularly like the "screen screen display of the main screen" there. Hey -- here's an idea. Let's translate this back into English!! HOORAY!!
