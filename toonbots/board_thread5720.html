Three Men in a Boat [Jerome K. Jerome] - 10.0

So we went to the bookstore the other day (a satisfying change from Ponce, of course, which has none, but the English-language selection is both limited and very pricey here) and I bought a book I'd been meaning to read for some time: Three Men in a Boat (To Say Nothing of the Dog), by Jerome K. Jerome. It's one of the quintessential humorous descriptions of Victorian England and I enjoyed it immensely.

Ah, the low-bandwidth life. It has its moments.
