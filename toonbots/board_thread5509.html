Dances With Wolverines

Still busy with grad school applications, though finally finishing up the entries for Jerry Beck's animated features book, due out some time next Fall in all likelihood. Still need to get Jenn's goodies together, and Michael needs to check his e-mail and get back to me on the Wodehouse titles.

In the meantime, the artistic muse has returned to me, and in honor of the time of the year, and celebrating their noble heritage, I shall be presenting to ye all who post with forked tongues images of the glorious wolverine, the so-called Devil of the North, in traditional Native American attire. (Besides, the horde got into my cache of John Ford movies, and have thus currently absorbed a passion for the West and for ancestral customs, in more ways than one, which only makes me glad that these were used VHS, and that DVDs are slightly tougher on the teeth, only in that they twist around and get stuck to the roof of their mouths, as Godfrey learned when he graciously volunteered to rid me of the HR Pufnstuf boxed set).

In any event, behold, in native splendlour, Alaric, demonstrating his manhood, bravery, and ability to look adorable in feathers.
