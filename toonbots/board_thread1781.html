Indeed, excellent Toonbots today. Wonderfully involved, and aside from the Scooby-Doo parody, I loved the Lone Ranger reference at the end. And of course Napoleon and Tirdun made splendid meta-cameos as "the pesky, meddling kids." Capital job.

However, one slight concern. I don't mean to seem overly fussy, but once again, in the last panel, we have another "NEXT: WOLVERINES" sign. Napoleon and I discussed this at the Con, and we find those signs to be misleading and increasingly frustrating. Please, I don't mean to offend, and due to the coding and so forth, while you may intend to include wolverines, sometimes it takes too long so you wait 'til next time. Fine, understandable, but don't you have at least a vague idea whether you'll be able to render wolverines yet or not? If not, please, stop raising our hopes, then! Or just forget about any "NEXT: WOLVERINES" notices, and just surprise us. I knoe you derive great pleasure from teasing your Jihad and frustrating their expectations, but we'd greatly appreciate it if you would reconsider your policy in regard to the wolverines.

And so would the wolverines. This continual disappointment has done little to gruntle them.
