Amazing

It's truly amazing that you're all still around after my dismal update performance of the last two months. You're all obviously a couple dogs short of a full pack, but you're a magnificent Jihad. Now that the Toon-o-Matic is back to (nearly) full functionality, I really hope to be able to get onto a more normal update schedule -- for me, this means from one to three days between, instead of (say) sixteen days. I just need ellipses now and I'm back in the black. Well, except I can't rotate text yet. But that'll come soon enough and it's not really all that critical.

There is a majordomo list now for auto (or almost auto) notification; feel free to tell me if you want on it (I don't have autosignup enabled, due to a turf war between sendmail and majordomo for directory permissions; it's a long and sordid story and I won't bore you with it.) If it's good enough for Emsworth, it's good enough for you. Or you can continue to click obsessively every day in the hope that I got an extra five frickin minutes to draw an episode.

Of course, if you *really* wanted more Toonbots, you'd come over here and babysit my kids while my wife and I went to the movies once in a while. This might not lead to more Toonbots, but I'd be happier.

On second thought, I'm not sure if I'd really trust a Toonbots fan with my kids...

Or vice versa.

In short, thanks for your support and you're all a credit to your ... um ... ethnic groupings, or species, whatever is appropriate.
