The metacartoonist reaches into the vast storehouse of our common Angloamerican heritage and pulls out Gilbert and Sullivan and Oz, oh my! Gilbert and Sullivan and Oz, oh my! *ahem* The metacartoonist reaches into the vast storehouse of our common heritage, as I was saying, thus illuminating the cognitive/artistic/symbolic medium with a panoply of semiotic allusive constructs, each hand-picked at the height of its freshness and lovingly arranged within the now-traditional Pokey the Penguin panel layout.
