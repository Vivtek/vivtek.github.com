Demise of old server

Well, my hosting provider of eight years decided to get out of the business. Naturally, juggler of too many things that I am, I didn't have time to get everything moved smoothly; one of the very low-priority items was this bulletin board. Which, you will doubtlessly note, runs a HELL OF A LOT FASTER on this spiffy new server.

Anyhoo, now that the holidays are over, and now that most of the other systems I had on the old server are running again (and all, of course, a HELL OF A LOT FASTER), I've taken pity and restored the bulletin board to its full functionality. Yes, yes, I'm sure the archives still don't work. And yes, I noticed that the *toon* archives aren't working either. Eventually the way will be made straight, and there will be rejoicing.

Actually, I didn't take pity at all. I just got really tired of seeing those errors in the log as all you lovely people continued to check whether I'd gotten my act together yet.
