EPISODE #100!
A rip-roaring ride through the wacky annals of Toonbottery, from the very first panels-only minimalist strip up to the present day with its regular use of fifth-wall humor, this episode is a masterfully interwoven tapestry of toon prowess and mild-mannered self-deprecating prankster-imbued joke/jest/social-commentary, and as such is fittingly the one-hundredth installment of the vanguard of minimalist tooning: Toonbots.
