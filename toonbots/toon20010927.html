Where did my Google go?
Toonbots archive - episode published 20010927


First toon    Previous toon    Next toon    Today's toon
Emboldened by the lack of flamage at the mere mention of Osama bin Laden, the metacartoonist begins the onerous task of making him the butt of humor. Far from simply jumping on the bandwagon of public opinion, the metacartoonist strives to put bin Laden into his proper iconic slot in the American psyche -- not as murderer of thousands, but as vaguely ridiculous posturing fascist, much as Charlie Chaplin in The Great Dictator.
