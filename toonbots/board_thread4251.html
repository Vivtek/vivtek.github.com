Book: _Storm Front_, by Jim Butcher: 10

A gritty detective novel set in Chicago, from the perspective of one Harry Blackstone Copperfield Dresden: wizard for hire. The only one in the Chicago Yellow Pages. A little like Harry Potter for grownups (although I must say that so far, Harry Potter #4 is an excellent read; just started it today).

The first by Butcher, it says it's the first in the new series "The Dresden Files" -- I certainly hope this will be the case, because it's an exceptionally excellent first novel. Well-written, suspenseful, full of vibrant and only slightly unbelievable characters: Dresden himself, Murphy the cop, mafioso Jimmy Marcone, a vampire (who runs a brothel), a demon or two, faeries, and a minivan-driving soccer mom. And the Bad Guy, of course. Hoo boy.

For all you webtoon junkies, this is a shoe-in. Read it.
