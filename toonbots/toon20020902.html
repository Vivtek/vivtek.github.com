PLEASE RETURN TO YOUR PLACES OF BUSINESS AND CONTINUE ABOUT YOUR DAILY ACTIVITIES UNTIL SUCH TIME AS YOU ARE NOTIFIED THAT THE UNITED ARMED PENGUIN FRONT HAS COMMENCED EXISTENCE AND EXPECTS YOUR UTTER AND SLAVISH OBEDIENCE IN ALL MATTERS, ESPECIALLY SEAFOOD-RELATED.
