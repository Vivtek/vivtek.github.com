IMPORTANT ANNOUNCEMENT: Toonbots leaving Vivtek

In the course of the next few days, Toonbots will be leaving Vivtek. This is not due to any quarrel with the company, with whom my relations have always been good. I simply wish, in the style of 1970s Fleetwood Mac, to go my own way. Though obviously without the copious cocaine abuse (stay away from gak, kids). The transition to a new server will take place at some point during the next couple of weeks, at which point this message board will be closed down and unfortunately you will have to register for a new one (sorry). Also, the mailing list will revert to me delivering the strips by hand, which has been known to be erratic (sorry).

I hope you will be along for this bold new age of independence. There may be a couple of days disruption but quality should remain as mediocre as you've come to expect!

Michael
