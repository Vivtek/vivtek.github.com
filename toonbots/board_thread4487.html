In memory of a Jihad member...

Eric Timothy Baars, known on the Toonbots forum as Sir Timber Bram, knight artificer of pixels, passed away on October 23 at the age of 34. He had been suffering from Hodgkin's for several years, and his most recent chemo treatments had not been going well (which is part of the reason he hadn't been posting in recent months.) Despite that, I had still hoped he might recover well enough to join Napoleon and I in San Diego. He was a Christian, was a computer technician or enginer by trade and also something of a grammarian, and lived in Canada. In as much as one can know a person strictly through online communication, he was a gentle man, polite and friendly and never one to contribute to flame wars but would firmly yet civilly put his foot down when needed. I first met him on the Class Menagerie forum in 2000, and eventually introduced him to Toonbots. He always maintained a positive attitude despite his condition, and had a good sense of humour, always amused by the wolverines and appreciative of their affectionate natures. It had been months since we had exchanged e-mails, though I had sent him a card earlier in October, which I can only hope he managed to see before he went.

Farewell, Tim. At last you are at peace, but you made an impact on my life, and on others as well, and we will never forget you.
