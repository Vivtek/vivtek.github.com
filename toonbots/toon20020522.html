Where did my Google go?
Toonbots archive - episode published 20020522


First toon    Previous toon    Next toon    Today's toon
Lord Emsworth's poignant attempt to delve deeper into the fragil psyche of Vladimir Ilyich Lenin leads to an enlightening discourse. The sweet strains of music stir within the soul of all humanity, as intellect and passion are merged into one ethereal unit. The occasional crashing of the maraca merely adds to the pathos, which is inherent in our ever contradictory existence, rendered even more lucid by the distant twang of the mandolin.
