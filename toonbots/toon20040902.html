Where did my Google go?
Toonbots archive - episode published 20040902


First toon    Previous toon    Next toon    Today's toon
The meteorological theme continues unabated, providing a trivial parallel with Frances' strength, while Mao's irritation with the childlike exuberance of the metafictional Frances ego/id-memetic pararepresentation points and counterpoints that parallel, the underlying script structure indicative of an amusing quasipositional posturing on the part of the metacartoonist, all this being of course a sort of tempest in a microcosmic teapot, itself a sublime commentary on the Republican National Convention in New York City, the currency boursen in Southeast Asia, and the ecological niches assumed by hermit crabs; the obvious conclusion to be drawn by the serious reader is of course that all things are both interconnected and essentially meaningless, that all meaning is a construct of our own fevered imaginations whistling past the graveyard of the immense vastness and silence of the physical cosmos.
