I'm in over my head

Well, as much as it pains me, I'm going to have to declare an official hiatus for a while (which, knowing me, probably means I'll be daily all through January.) I've just got too much real work that's not getting done and even though I don't spend much time with Toonbots, I need to go cold-turkey on online comics for a while.

BUT. Since this is Toonbots, I hope that my "hiatus" will simply be a phase change to Something Completely Different.

Within a month, if all goes well, I'll have the time to put the Toon-o-Matic online, meaning that Toonbots can become a truly collaborative venture. In that spirit, I will henceforth accept submissions, starting now, for further episodes from all titled Jihad members, and these episodes will be part of the official canon. Particularly good ideas will earn titles for non-titled people. Submissions may be ideas, blurb text, or real XML. Please use this forum to hash things out. An episode will not be considered complete until it has working XML (I'll compile it and debug it) and a blurb, and I will help out as time and energy allow. Any titled Jihad member has a line-item veto over the storyline, and of course, if I decide to rewrite things, I WILL. Because I'm still the metacartoonist, dammit.

At the moment this system will be operated manually, but what the hell, the reason I'm so damned busy is that I'm writing a workflow-oriented content management system anyway -- so by, oh, next Defenestration Day I'd like to have it fully automated.

But I can't keep things up the way they're going, and this will remove one of the teeny-tiny little stress sources I can actually control. Don't disappoint me. Give me episodes. You've got that whole hostile-takeover story arc, you could figure out who's going to be Officer Obie, the whole CommieCon thing really never got resolved, the wolverines have yet to be drawn, N.A.P.O.L.E.O.N. and Ilya are still after Lenin, and the metacartoonist will not only probably never escape, he is now under the remote control of the Jihad. This has a great deal of possibility, if you all step up to the plate and help out.

The author(s) of each episode will be named in the blurb. I really want this to work out. Especially since Tirdun did Lenin's little dream sequence back in July, this has been my secret ambition, and in the last couple of months, the tone of the forum has really changed and matured; you've become a real (if small) community and I think it's time you all had the chance to be Real Live Metacartoonists. Or at least, being metacartoonists in the same sense as John Cusack was John Malkovitch. Yeah. That's got *real* possibilities.

Pretty please?
