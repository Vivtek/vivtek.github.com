Of all the toons in all the world, BoxJam, you had to cameo in mine.

With an absolutely astounding display of virtuosity, the metacartoonist rings in the New Year with real, live, non-bitmapped, honest-to-God, drawing. Drawing. This, my friends, will be the Year of the Toonbot, as the Toon-o-Matic takes us to ever more complex realms of cartooning splendor.
