Where did my Google go?
Toonbots archive - episode published 20061029


First toon    Previous toon    Next toon    Today's toon
This astonishing episode embodies what can only be called metanarrative -- starting from the already groundbreaking narrative structure of the previous episode, the metacartoonist performs a trivial (yet simultaneously masterful) rearrangement of panels in preparation for the upcoming tour de force associated with the reintroduction of text into the minimalist paradigm take 2; this current episode is a whimsical presentation of that very action, that very change in the narrative structure. This blurring of the distinction between the action timeline of the cartoon's own narrative structure and the action timeline of the creation of the cartoon is one the metacartoonist is absolutely positive has never before been considered, the very notion being sufficient to exclude the comprehension of beings lesser than those doubtlessly now enthralled by this new and heady height of metacartooning.
