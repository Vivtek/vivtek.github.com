Intacto - 9.5

Intacto is a Spanish movie and was dubbed (into English, at least) somewhat halfheartedly. I couldn't decide whether this harmed the film or added to its camp value. Probably a little of both. But I wish I had seen it with subtitles instead -- there's a lot to be said for hearing the actual actors' voices with the intended intonation.

Anyway, it was a good flick and the premise was pretty decent. I really enjoyed the cabalistic-magic flavor of the whole thing. My wife, being a physicist, simply found it relatively incomprehensible -- but still enjoyed it.

Worth a watch. Or even some other timepiece.
