10/17 Spoiler (Cameo!)

Well, been a bit preoccupied lately, and probably won't be posting at all for awhile, but I'm thoroughly enjoying the current minimalist plot direction. Butch's cameo was quite unexpected, and frankly he might be just the one to deal with the current election mess. Really nice, trademark expression you've chosen. I've always felt that the worried, thoughtful expression Mr. Herold gives to his creation somehow makes Butch rather sympathetic, a lost soul attempting to find his way in the world. Perhaps by finally embracing the pure simplicity of the Toon-o-Matic and falling under the guidance of the wise and benevolent dot and bot, he may finally find true peace.

Political commentary, a nice rhyme scheme, more of the great gopherine dot, and a cameo to boot! My, you just keep churning them out this week, don't you, Mr. Roberts?

I look forward to what new heights Toonbots might achieve in the near future (though frankly, I'm quite content right now!) (By the way, I hope you don't mind my asking, but I'm one of the few who has yet to receive a title from the PAFTWJ. As the one to post the first Toonbots spoiler, would you mind terribly if I selected "Spontaneous Spoiler Specialist" as my title of choice?)

Well, goodnight all (and my sympathies for the High Priest.)
