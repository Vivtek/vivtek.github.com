The, um, well, the metacartoonist is at his usual loss of words when it comes to early misfires of any Toon-o-Matic changes, in this case the re-addition of character drawing into the Toon-o-Matic toolkit. Of course, a sufficiently perspicacious reader will have no difficulting in discerning the manifold ways in which this episode, superficially flawed though it is, in reality reflects a much deeper level of perfection approaching the ineffable, that deeper level being the level always considered by the metacartoonist to be his true canvas; the actual arrangement of pixels upon your screen, be that arrangement ever so precisely determined by the ineluctible workings of the Toon-o-Matic's definitional clockwork, can only be a pale, wan indication of the true meaning of the actual concept it embodies -- this, of course, is always the case in any artistic endeavor (that artistry of course even including the composition of the meanest shopping list or telegram), but the very imperfections of the current actual version of the Toon-o-Matic code vis-a-vis the theoretical Toon-o-Matic which all current versions must perforce approximate serve especially to highlight those very vicissitudes of expressive failure, thus making the Toon-o-Matic, and by extension, Toonbots as a whole and even, Dear Reader, you, a powerful and unique symbol of the set of all possible universes of discourse.
