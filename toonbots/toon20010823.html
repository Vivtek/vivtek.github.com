Where did my Google go?
Toonbots archive - episode published 20010823


First toon    Previous toon    Next toon    Today's toon
The metacartoonist ushers in a surprising new thrust in minimalist cartooning with a quick cameo of Pokey the Penguin, in utter defiance of all established principles of copyright. Intellectual property, of course, is a bourgeois invention of the running-dog lackeys of corporate America, and as such is utterly inconsequential to any serious intellectual endeavor in the new millenium.

Oh, all right. Pokey is here. Try not to hurt your brain.
