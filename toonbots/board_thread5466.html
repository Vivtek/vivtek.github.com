Avast and oho! Tis a merry little bit of booty which has been brought forth on decks before us this voyage. Aye, the use of vernacular and dialect, particularly that related to the noble profession of nautical re-acquisitionist, blows through the mainsails like a right bonny zephyr says I. Belay all the plans fo the new vessel to be christened SCUMF for the moment, this old sea-beast be up to many a new cruise on her own, to the alarm and delight of tipsy merchant marines. Aye, arrr, arf, vast, and swashing the buckles to mast the pivot of the tiller and yo ho ho and merrily we roll along!
