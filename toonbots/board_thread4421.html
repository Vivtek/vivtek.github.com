Musings

A sense of uncontrollably helpless euphoria surges through one's veins. Joy and merriment fill every fiber of one's being. Every boulevard and avenue of the mind is filled with merry and bright figures gadding about, whistling sprightly tunes, and ambrosial scents waft through one's delighted nostrils. Sheer rapture surrounds one, and one is engulfed in the shower of delights and ecstasy, from which there is no escape. The butterflies advance inexorably upon one, and the rains of sweet nectar shall soon drown one.
